## üìä Dashboards Included

### 1Ô∏è‚É£ Orders, Payments & Product Analysis Dashboard
This dashboard focuses on understanding how customers place orders and pay for products.

**Key Insights:**
- Credit cards are the dominant payment method, accounting for ~74% of total orders.
- Boleto and voucher payments contribute significantly but remain secondary options.
- Home & living and health & beauty categories generate the highest number of orders.
- Most customers prefer fewer payment installments, with single-installment payments being the most common.
- Time-based filters (Year, Quarter, Month) enable trend analysis across periods.

**Visuals Used:**
- Bar chart: Orders by payment type
- Bar chart: Orders by product category
- Table: Payment installments distribution
- Slicers: Year, Quarter, Month
